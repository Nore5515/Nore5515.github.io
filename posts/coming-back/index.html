Been a while, huh? I had a lot happen in the past few years. I got this blog working once again and looking back, I realize how little I actually knew back then. Now, nearly two years later, I am smarter and realize how much I don't know. Which is a lot.

I don't have much to say at the moment. One of my favorite bloggers usually does this thing where he writes a huuuuuge opening paragraph, a rambling opening that's just him free writing. He says it helps him clear his mind before writing an article and it makes it easier, so I guess I should try.

That's also what I just did. Works out!

Stay tuned for...whatever I end up posting on here. Or don't. I'm a blog, not a cop.
